Common questions

Before you book, a few common ones.

What does an engagement cost?
It depends on scope — and that's the honest answer. After a 30-minute discovery call, we send a written proposal within 48 hours: fixed fee, clear timeline, no hidden costs. You decide whether it works. No pressure, no follow-up calls.
How long do engagements typically run?
Project-based work usually runs four to eight weeks, depending on scope. Retainers are month-to-month — start when you need us, pause when you don't, with 30 days' notice. We never lock you into long contracts.
Do you sign NDAs before the discovery call?
Yes — happily. Email hello@syndavo.com with "NDA" in the subject and we'll send a mutual NDA the same business day. We also accept reasonable third-party NDAs without negotiation. Even without paperwork, confidentiality is our default from the first call onward.
Is everything remote?
Yes. We're fully remote and async-first by design — no offices, no travel costs charged to you, no calendar Tetris. Synchronous calls when they earn their place; async writing and reviewing for everything else. We work across your time zone, never against it.
What happens after our first call?
Within 48 hours, you receive a written proposal: scope, deliverables, timeline, fee, and the team. You either accept it, send back a revision, or walk away — there's no pressure either direction. If you accept, we start within a week.
We're at idea stage with no team — are we too early?
Idea-stage is exactly where we do our best work. The earlier we engage, the more leverage strategy creates — before assumptions get baked into product decisions and before runway gets spent on the wrong things. You don't need a team, a deck, or a deadline. You need an idea worth pursuing.
